Civic Connect - Terms & Conditions

By signing up, you agree to follow these guidelines to keep our community safe and transparent.

CivicConnect enables citizens to report civic issues such as waste mismanagement, traffic congestion, water scarcity, and pollution. It provides issue tracking so users can monitor status updates. Our goal is to build a transparent, collaborative community.

  • Register & Profile: Become an CivicConnect user.
  • Report Issues: Share details, photos & locations of problems.
  • Track Reports: Follow progress of issues you or others raise.
  • Engage: Support, upvote, and discuss issues relevant to your area.

  • Truthful Reporting: Share only accurate and genuine information.
  • Self-Owned Submissions: You are responsible for all uploads.
  • Respectful Communication: No abusive or offensive language.
  • No Misuse: Avoid personal attacks or irrelevant content.

CivicConnect ensures fair visibility of issues and a bridge between citizens and authorities.
Note: Resolution may take time, but we prioritize transparency.
Data Privacy: Your personal details remain secure.

By registering, you confirm:
  • Your information is accurate & genuine.
  • You accept full responsibility for reports submitted.
  • You will follow CivicConnect’s community standards.
